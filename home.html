Employment and Entrepreneurship Fair 2022

The Employment and Entrepreneurship Fair began as an initiative of the Directory of the Alumni Network of the UPB university in 2020. The objective of the fair is to support the employability of our students, graduates and the population in general, in addition to contributing to the economic reactivation of the country promoting entrepreneurship in our community

2022.12.15(THU) ~ 16 (FRI)

@Campus Julio Leon Prado, UPB

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various countries about employment and entrepreneurship issues

Job Matching

Share your CV and concrete job interviews with the best companies in the country

Business Roundtables

Schedules meetings between suppliers and demanders for the purpose of holding interviews and promoting contacts between them for business and associative relationships
